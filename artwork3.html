"double trouble"

Adobe Photoshop, Audacity, 2020

2880 x 1624

This artwork was created in my ART 74 class for both my digital image composition and glitch art assignment. I was inspired to recreate the Spiderman meme to match our current circumstances (i.e global pandemic, wildfires, overall hysteria). Using Adobe Photoshop, I extended the background and distanced the characters at approximately six feet, added props such as the USA sticker, masks, a hand sanitizer dispenser, and the pneumatic launcher, also known as the "Mask Gun", by Allen Pan. To add the glitch effects, I used Audacity and mainly utilized the effects "echo", "invert", and "distort", emphasizing the caption and background.
