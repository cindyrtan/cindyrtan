Introduction

Posted by Cindy on September 3, 2020

Hi there! My name is Cindy Tran and I am currently 17 years old. I am a first year student at San Jose State University majoring in Digital Media Art and minoring in Interaction Design. I am born and raised in San Jose, CA and enjoy playing volleyball, doing arts and crafts, and cooking in my spare time. A lot of my art experience comes from my childhood, being self-taught while obtaining inspiration from reading children's books, watching cartoons, and anime. I look forward to learning beyond traditional art and meeting other San Jose State Spartans!
