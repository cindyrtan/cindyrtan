"silent heart"

Audacity, 2020

1457 x 1800

I wanted to incorporated Qing Han's (qinniart) last artwork in my glitch art piece before she passed away on February 8, 2020. I primarily used the distort, echo, inverse, and normalize effects from Audacity to illustrate how the world can be, and is, full of imperfections and toxicity. I admire how the effects turned the character's tears into "blood", adding a darker theme, and covering her mouth to illustrate how one's voice can be muffled within the chaos of the world.
