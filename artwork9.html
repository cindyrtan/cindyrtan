"rich bear"

❮ ❯

Meshmixer, Adobe Dimension, 2020

1024 x 768

My 3D art remix was created using three object files from Poly by Google: a teddy bear, a female torso, and a toilet. I went into this assignment without a plan. I explored different resources and bookmarked certain files that interested me. I looked at each object and tried to imagine and rearrange the parts inside my head, in which I ended up creating the absurd 3D sculpture I have now. After some tweaking and troubleshooting, I decided to add some more details to the sculpture by utilizing Dimension's presets and basic models. Upon first glance, the final product appears sophisticated but the more you observe, the more oddly humorous and perplexing it looks. This piece is what I would call a “mysterious yet comical mistake”. The sculpture is intended to be open to interpretation as it could represent various concepts such as the beauty of maximalism, depictiion of the strange tastes of wealthy people, or internal human greed and foolishness.

Original File Sources: Torso, Toilet, Teddy Bear
