"skin care"

❮ ❯

Canva, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Dimension, 2020

1526 x 1134

As a skincare enthusiast, I wanted to create a 3D model/render of a mockup skincare line with the concepts and ingredients being inspired by real existing products. This personal project of mine was fun to execute and gave me a taste of what graphic and product design would be like. The labels were created using Canva and Photoshop, while the scene and objects were from Adobe Dimension.

Product Details:



"nice rice" is made with micro-ground rice powder and infused with rice bran water. This foaming cleanser gently exfoliates the skin, removing dirt, oil and sebum, while visibly brightening for exceptionally soft skin. Apply a dime size and emulsify with water, then gently massage into the skin and wash off with lukewarm water.



"skin milk" is made with white leaf tea water for softening and intense moisturizing for dry and dehydrated skin. A milky yet quick-absorbing 2-in-1 hybrid that softens like a toner and hydrates like a moisturizer. After cleansing, apply desired amount over face for instant hydration.



"halo serum" is made with hyaluronic acid, niacinamide, linden flower and white tea leaf extract. A daily moisture-boosting and quick-absorbing serum that instantly hydrates, replenishes and smooths appearances of fine and dry lines. Suitable for all skin types. After cleansing and before moisturizing, apply a few drops onto damp skin and pat gently. Use morning & night for best results.



"dew cream" is made with azulene, centella asiatica, & spirulina. Designed to care for skin damaged from environmental stressors like UV exposure and pollution. Suitable for all skin types. A jelly-like yet cooling gel cream that effortlessly revitalizes and hydrates dull skin. Apply desired amount as the last step of skincare routine for dewy and glowing skin. Use as a cooling mask by keeping the product refrigerated prior to use.
